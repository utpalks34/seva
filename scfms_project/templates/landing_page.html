About SCFMS

Understanding our vision and commitment

Smart Governance for Modern India

The Smart City Feedback Management System (SCFMS) is a digital initiative under the Digital India Program designed to transform how citizens and governments interact.

We bridge communication gaps through transparency, empower citizens with voice, and equip government officials with AI-driven insights for smarter decision-making.
